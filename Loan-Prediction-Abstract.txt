The csv(comma seperated value) file contains the records of loan recipient. 
There are no. of columns in the file. 
Each column in the file represents attributes and information of various people applied for loan in a bank.
The final column i.e Loan Status is checked against all the columns and machine is trained and tested.  
Loan status holds T or F where T signifies Applicable and F signifies Not Applicable
After machine is trained and tested, prediction can be made.
Input the information of loan applicant as per the columns who wish to take loan from the bank.
The machine processes the information and give output as T or F.
